# Activity Tracker

A simple web app to log daily activities and sync them with Google Drive. Track what you've done, remember what you're waiting on, and get weekly summaries—all searchable.

## Features

- **Log activities** with details, dates, and notes
- **Track waiting items** with reminders of who/what you're waiting on
- **Search by activity name** to quickly find what you need
- **Weekly reports** showing completed tasks and items you're waiting on
- **Google Drive sync** to access your tracker from any device
- **Cross-device access** — use it on your phone, tablet, or desktop

## How It Works

1. Sign in with Google
2. Your activities sync automatically to Google Drive
3. Access the same data from any device

## Deployment

This app is deployed on Vercel and runs entirely in your browser.

## Privacy

- All data is stored in your Google Drive
- Your credentials are handled by Google's secure authentication
- No data is stored on any server except your Google Drive
